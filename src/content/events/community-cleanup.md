## Community Cleanup Drive

We're organizing a community cleanup drive to maintain the cleanliness of our beautiful town. All members and community members are welcome to participate.

### Details
- **Date:** December 15, 2025
- **Time:** 8:00 AM - 12:00 PM
- **Location:** Baddegama Central Park
- **Activities:** Waste collection, tree planting, area beautification

### What to Bring
- Comfortable shoes
- Gloves and bags
- Water bottle
- Sun protection

Join us in making Baddegama a cleaner and greener place!
