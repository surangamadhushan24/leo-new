## Tree Plantation Drive - Growing Green Together

Join our Leo Club for a meaningful environmental initiative aimed at increasing green coverage in our community and combating climate change.

### Event Overview
We believe every tree planted today is a legacy for tomorrow. This tree plantation drive brings together community members, volunteers, and environmental enthusiasts to make a tangible difference.

### Details
- **Date:** March 20, 2026
- **Time:** 9:00 AM - 1:00 PM
- **Location:** Baddegama Botanical Garden
- **Target:** Plant 500+ trees

### What We'll Do
- Select and prepare native tree species
- Plant trees in designated areas
- Learn about environmental conservation
- Network with fellow volunteers
- Enjoy refreshments post-activity

### What to Bring
- Comfortable clothing and shoes (outdoor activity)
- A hat and sunscreen
- Water bottle
- Work gloves (optional - we'll provide them)
- Your enthusiasm for environmental work!

### Why Participate?
- Contribute to environmental conservation
- Build community bonds
- Learn about sustainable practices
- Make a lasting impact in Baddegama
- Earn volunteer hours

**Registration Required:** Limited spots available. Please register at the event venue or contact us in advance.
