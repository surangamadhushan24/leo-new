## Student Scholarship Program Launch

We're proud to announce the launch of our annual scholarship program dedicated to supporting deserving students in our community.

### Event Details
This is a significant event in our calendar as we announce scholarships for deserving students who demonstrate academic excellence and financial need.

### Program Highlights
- **Total Scholarships:** 15 awards
- **Value per Scholarship:** LKR 25,000
- **Focus:** Academic excellence & community contribution
- **Application Deadline:** March 15, 2026

### Event Schedule
- 10:00 AM - Welcome & Introductions
- 10:30 AM - Leo Club Mission Presentation
- 11:00 AM - Scholarship Details & Eligibility Criteria
- 12:00 PM - Q&A Session
- 12:30 PM - Lunch
- 1:30 PM - Application Support Booth

### Who Should Attend?
- Prospective scholarship applicants (students)
- Parents and guardians
- Educators and school representatives
- Community members interested in education support

### Eligibility Criteria
- Currently enrolled in Grade 11 or 12
- Strong academic performance (GPA 3.5+)
- Financial need demonstrated
- Active participation in community service

### Required Documents
- Academic transcripts
- Income certificate
- Personal statement
- Letters of recommendation

Join us as we invest in the future of our community through education!
