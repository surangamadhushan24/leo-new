## Community Library Setup - Knowledge for All

We successfully established a community library in Baddegama to provide free access to educational resources for all community members, particularly underprivileged students.

## Objectives
- Create equitable access to educational materials
- Support student learning beyond school curriculum
- Develop reading culture in the community
- Support children's literacy development
- Provide a safe study space for students

## Project Timeline
- **Phase 1** (July): Site selection and renovation
- **Phase 2** (August): Book collection and cataloging
- **Phase 3** (September): Official launch and training
- **Phase 4** (Ongoing): Library management and maintenance

## Achievements
- Secured 2,000+ books across various subjects
- Recruited and trained 10 volunteer librarians
- Established sustainable library management system
- Created structured reading programs for children
- Achieved 50+ active borrowers in first month

## Impact Metrics
- **Books Available:** 2,000+
- **Weekly Visitors:** 50+ students
- **Book Circulation:** 200+ books per month
- **Volunteer Hours:** 400+ hours contributed
- **Community Members Impacted:** 500+

## Ongoing Activities
- Daily reading sessions for children
- Monthly book club discussions
- Homework support center
- Literacy programs for families
- Author exchange programs with schools

This project represents our commitment to educational accessibility and community development.
