## සුභාවිත‘25,'
 organized by the Leo Club of Baddegama for Literacy and Reading Month, was a national-level literacy competition promoting reading, writing, and creative thinking among youth. Featuring poems, free verse, novel reviews, and short stories, it received nearly 80 submissions nationwide. The event included a Song Review Programme. Leo members managed all logistics, ensuring a memorable, innovative celebration of literacy, culture, and youth engagement
