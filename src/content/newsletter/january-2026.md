## Welcome to 2026!

As we step into the new year, BG Leos is energized and ready to make an even greater impact in our community. This newsletter brings you the latest updates, success stories, and what to expect in the coming months.

## Recent Achievements

### Community Cleanup Success
Our January community cleanup initiative was a huge success! Over 50 volunteers participated in cleaning 5km of roads around Baddegama. Thanks to everyone who contributed their time and effort.

### New Member Orientation
We welcomed 12 new members to the Leo family this month. Our orientation program covered the Leo mission, available projects, and leadership opportunities. Welcome aboard!
