## November Highlights

As we approach the end of the year, November has been a significant month for BG Leos. Our community engagement reached new heights with several impactful initiatives.

## Community Outreach

### Youth Mentorship Program
This month, we launched our expanded youth mentorship program, pairing 25 young mentees with experienced Leo mentors. The program focuses on leadership development, career guidance, and personal growth.

### Health Awareness Campaign
Our health and wellness team conducted awareness sessions in three schools, reaching approximately 200 students. Topics covered included mental health awareness, nutrition, and physical fitness.

## Featured Project: Tree Planting Initiative

In celebration of World Environment Day, we organized a tree planting drive that brought together over 60 volunteers. We planted 150 saplings across Baddegama, contributing to a greener future.

**Impact Metrics:**
- 150 trees planted
- 60 volunteers engaged
- 8 locations covered
