## 2025 - A Year of Impact

What an incredible year it has been! As we close out 2025, we want to thank every member, supporter, and volunteer who made our achievements possible.

## 2025 by the Numbers

- **42** Projects Completed
- **36** Events Organized
- **500+** Lives Impacted
- **125,000 LKR** Raised for Charity

## Highlights from 2025

### Environmental Initiatives
Our commitment to environmental protection resulted in 10 major cleanup campaigns, tree planting drives, and awareness programs reaching over 200 community members.

### Youth Development
Through our mentorship and leadership programs, we guided 45 young people in their personal and professional growth journey.

### Community Partnerships
We strengthened our partnerships with 8 local organizations, amplifying our reach and impact across Baddegama.

## Looking Forward to 2026

The new year brings fresh opportunities and ambitious goals. We're planning:
- Expansion of our mentorship program
- New community projects in education and health
- Monthly wellness and networking events
- International Leo collaboration initiatives

## Gratitude

Thank you to our dedicated members, supportive families, community partners, and all volunteers. Your support makes our mission possible!
